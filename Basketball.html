All you need to know about Basketball!

Basketball was invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith in Springfield, Massachusetts, as a way to keep athletes active indoors during the winter. The original game used a soccer ball and two peach baskets as goals. Over time, the rules evolved, the sport grew rapidly, and professional leagues were formed, including the NBA in 1946. Today, basketball is one of the most popular sports worldwide because it can be played almost anywhere and requires only a ball and a hoop. The sport is known for its fast pace, athleticism, teamwork, and global fan community.

Basketball Rules:

  • Each team has 5 players on the court at a time.
  • The main objective is to score points by shooting the ball through the opponent’s hoop.
  • Players cannot run while holding the ball; they must dribble or pass to move.
  • A standard game is divided into four quarters (NBA: 12 minutes each, NCAA: 10 minutes each).
  • Shot clock: Teams must attempt a shot within 24 seconds (NBA) or 30 seconds (college).
  • Fouls: (reaching, pushing, charging, etc.) can result in free throws or turnovers.
  • Out of bounds: Possession goes to the other team when the ball leaves the court’s boundary lines.
  • Violations: such as traveling, double dribbling, or carrying result in the ball being turned over to the opposing team.
